Mindfulness in every pixel. A fluid, low-latency mobile experience.

> SCOPE

Engineered a cross-platform mindfulness application focused on reducing cognitive load through a highly constrained, minimalist UI and breath-synced micro-interactions.

[ DEPLOY LIVE APP ]

01. REDUCING COGNITIVE LOAD

When users open a mindfulness app, they are usually overwhelmed or stressed. The architecture of EZ Breathe actively fights "app fatigue." There are no complex onboarding menus or pop-ups. The app launches directly into the core breathing mechanic, utilizing pure black backgrounds to save battery on OLED screens and reduce eye strain.

02. 60FPS FLUID ANIMATION

A breathing app fails if the visual cues stutter. I bypassed standard DOM rendering and utilized hardware-accelerated graphics to ensure the expansion and contraction rings scale flawlessly at a locked 60 frames per second, syncing perfectly with the device's native haptic feedback.
